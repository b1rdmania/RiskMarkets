OVERVIEW

ECV measures volatility across energy corridors.

It reacts to changes in oil, gas, shipping, metals, and safe-haven flows.

It is a synthetic volatility index, not a geopolitical forecast.

WHY THIS INDEX MATTERS

Energy routes move fast when global conditions shift.

ECV packages these moves into a single neutral index traders can long or short.

HOW IT WORKS

  • Deployed as a HIP-3 perpetual on Hyperliquid
  • Built from neutral market feeds
