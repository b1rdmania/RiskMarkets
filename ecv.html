ECV — Energy Corridor Volatility

A synthetic index tracking volatility across global energy supply routes.

OVERVIEW

ECV measures volatility across major energy corridors using neutral market feeds.

It reacts to changes in oil, gas, shipping, safe-haven flows, and metals.

It is a synthetic macro index, not a geopolitical predictor.

INDEX COMPOSITION

INDEX COMPONENTS

  • 35% WTI (Pyth feed)
  • 20% Natural Gas (Pyth feed)
  • 20% Global Freight Proxy
  • 15% Gold (Pyth feed)
  • 10% USD/CHF (Pyth feed)

UPDATE CYCLE

2.5 seconds

FALLBACK LOGIC

Median of last 3 updates

HIP-3 STATUS

Compliant

STRESS LEVEL

STRESS LEVEL: MEDIUM

WHY THIS INDEX MATTERS

Energy corridors experience sudden volatility driven by supply shifts, shipping delays, and safe-haven demand.

ECV provides a neutral, packaged way to trade these movements using transparent components.

TRADING MODEL

  • ECV is deployed as a HIP-3 perpetual market on Hyperliquid
  • Users long/short via Hyperliquid's front end
  • WAR.MARKET provides the themed UI layer
  • Liquidity and execution remain fully Hyperliquid-native
