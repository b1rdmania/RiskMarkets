WHAT IS $RISK?

$RISK is the token that links the indices, the vault, and the points system.

It is a fee-share and incentive token.

UTILITY

  • Earn a share of protocol fees when staked
  • Boost points multipliers
  • Unlock early access to new indices

No governance theatre

No meme promises

Tied directly to usage of the markets

POINTS

Points track how much you use the system.

They are earned from:

  • Trading volume
  • Open interest held over time
  • Short exposure when the market is one-sided
  • Staking HYPE in the riskHYPE vault

CONVERSION

At TGE, points convert into $RISK.

More points → larger share of the initial $RISK allocation.

HOW IT FITS

  • • Trade indices → earn points
  • • Stake HYPE → earn more points
  • • Stake $RISK → earn fee-share
  • • Points → $RISK at launch

One simple loop:

Trade and stake → collect points → become an owner of the risk engine.
